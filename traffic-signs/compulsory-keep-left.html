Compulsory Keep Left Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

A blue circular sign giving a positive command: you must keep to the left. Blue mandatory signs tell you what you must do, unlike red signs that prohibit.

Shape & colour

Blue circle with a white arrow pointing left/down. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

At traffic islands and medians where traffic must pass on the left.

What to do

Keep to the left of the island or divider as directed.

Common test mistake

Ignoring it because it is not red — blue command signs are equally compulsory.
