Right Hairpin Bend Sign

Cautionary (warning) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

Warns of a very sharp right-hand bend ahead, common on ghat and hill roads.

Shape & colour

Red-bordered triangle showing a sharp right hairpin. Cautionary (warning) sign.

Where you'll see it

On winding hill and ghat roads in Kerala's high ranges.

What to do

Reduce speed well before the bend, stay in your lane, and use a low gear on descents.

Common test mistake

Braking hard inside the bend instead of slowing before it.
