Stop Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

The Stop sign requires you to bring your vehicle to a complete halt at the stop line. You may proceed only after stopping fully and making sure the way is clear and it is safe to continue.

Shape & colour

Red octagon with the word STOP in white. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

At junctions, level crossings and places where cross-traffic has priority or visibility is poor.

What to do

Stop completely at the stop line, look in all directions, give way to traffic on the main road, then move off when safe.

Common test mistake

Slowing down without fully stopping — the test (and the law) treat a rolling stop as not stopping at all.
