Bus Stop Sign

Informatory sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

An informatory sign marking a bus stop ahead where buses halt and passengers board or alight.

Shape & colour

Blue rectangle with a bus symbol. Informatory sign.

Where you'll see it

At designated bus stops along the road.

What to do

Expect buses to slow and stop; watch for pedestrians around the stop.

Common test mistake

Overtaking a stopped bus without checking for people crossing in front of it.
