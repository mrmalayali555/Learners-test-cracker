Narrow Bridge Ahead Sign

Cautionary (warning) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

Warns that a narrow bridge is ahead where the road width reduces and only limited vehicles can pass at a time.

Shape & colour

Red-bordered triangle showing a narrowing bridge. Cautionary (warning) sign.

Where you'll see it

Before bridges narrower than the approaching road.

What to do

Slow down, judge oncoming traffic, and give way if the other vehicle is closer.

Common test mistake

Assuming you have priority — whoever is nearer the bridge usually goes first.
