One-Way Traffic Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

The One-Way sign shows that traffic on that road flows in a single direction, indicated by the arrow.

Shape & colour

Blue rectangle with a white arrow. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

At the entry of one-way roads and lanes.

What to do

Drive only in the arrow's direction; never against it.

Common test mistake

Mixing it up with No Entry, which marks the forbidden end of a one-way road.
