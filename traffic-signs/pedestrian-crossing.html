Pedestrian Crossing Sign

Cautionary (warning) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

This warning sign indicates a pedestrian crossing ahead where people may be crossing the road.

Shape & colour

Red-bordered triangle with a pedestrian on a crossing. Cautionary (warning) sign.

Where you'll see it

Before zebra crossings in towns and near markets and schools.

What to do

Slow down and give way to anyone on or stepping onto the crossing.

Common test mistake

Confusing the warning triangle with the blue 'pedestrian crossing' information square.
