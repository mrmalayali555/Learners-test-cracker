No Entry Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

The No Entry sign means vehicles are prohibited from entering that road or lane in the direction you are facing. It is commonly used at the exit end of one-way streets.

Shape & colour

Red circle with a horizontal white bar. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

At the wrong-way end of one-way roads and at restricted entrances.

What to do

Do not enter. Find an alternative route.

Common test mistake

Assuming it only applies to cars — No Entry applies to all vehicles unless a plate says otherwise.
