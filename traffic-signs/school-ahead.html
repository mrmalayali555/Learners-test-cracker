School Ahead Sign

Cautionary (warning) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

A warning sign alerting you that a school is ahead and children may be crossing. Extra caution is essential.

Shape & colour

Red-bordered triangle with two children. Cautionary (warning) sign.

Where you'll see it

Before schools and areas with heavy child pedestrian activity.

What to do

Slow right down, watch for children, and be ready to stop.

Common test mistake

Speeding up to pass quickly — school zones need the slowest, most careful driving.
