Hospital Sign

Informatory sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

An informatory sign showing that a hospital is nearby. It guides you and reminds you to keep noise low.

Shape & colour

Blue rectangle with an 'H' symbol. Informatory sign.

Where you'll see it

On approaches to hospitals.

What to do

Drive quietly and carefully; avoid unnecessary horn use.

Common test mistake

Ignoring the linked silence zone and honking near the hospital.
