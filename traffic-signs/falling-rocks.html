Falling Rocks Sign

Cautionary (warning) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

Warns that rocks or debris may fall onto the road from the hillside ahead.

Shape & colour

Red-bordered triangle showing rocks falling down a slope. Cautionary (warning) sign.

Where you'll see it

On cut-hill roads and ghat sections, especially after rain.

What to do

Stay alert, avoid stopping under the slope, and watch for debris on the carriageway.

Common test mistake

Parking or halting directly beneath the rock-fall zone.
