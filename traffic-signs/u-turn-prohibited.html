U-Turn Prohibited Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

The U-Turn Prohibited sign means you must not make a U-turn at that location.

Shape & colour

Red circle with a U-turn arrow crossed out. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

At busy junctions, medians and stretches where turning back would be dangerous.

What to do

Continue and take the next permitted turning point instead.

Common test mistake

Confusing it with 'right turn prohibited' — check whether the crossed-out arrow is a U-shape.
