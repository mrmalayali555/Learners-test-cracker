Give Way Sign

Mandatory (regulatory) sign · Kerala learners licence test

Meaning

The Give Way sign tells you to slow down, be ready to stop, and give priority to traffic on the road you are joining or crossing. You do not always have to stop, but you must yield.

Shape & colour

Downward-pointing triangle with a red border on white. Mandatory (regulatory) sign.

Where you'll see it

Where a minor road meets a major road, and at roundabouts and some junctions.

What to do

Slow down, watch traffic on the priority road, and proceed only when there is a safe gap.

Common test mistake

Confusing Give Way with Stop — Give Way lets you keep moving if the way is clear; Stop always needs a full halt.
