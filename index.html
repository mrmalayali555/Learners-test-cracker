Kerala Learners Test — Frequently Asked Questions

How many questions are in the Kerala learners licence test?

RTO Cracker's mock test has 30 multiple-choice questions, 30 seconds each, and you must answer at least 18 correctly to pass. Questions come from traffic signs, road rules, and driving safety topics.

Is RTO Cracker free to use?

Yes — all 827 practice questions, sign images, topic-wise practice, and unlimited mock tests are completely free.

What topics should I study for the learners test?

Traffic signs, hand signals and police signals, road markings, rules of the road, safe driving practices, fines and penalties under the Motor Vehicles Act, and licence & vehicle documents — RTO Cracker covers the full syllabus topic by topic.

How do I pass the learners test on the first attempt?

Practice topic by topic until your accuracy is high, then take mock tests until you consistently score 18+ out of 30. Pay special attention to traffic sign questions — they make up the biggest share of the exam.
