How to Get Your Kerala Learners Licence (LLR) — Step-by-Step Guide

Obtaining a Kerala learners licence (LLR) is the first step towards driving legally on public roads. This guide outlines the process, eligibility, required documents, application steps, and validity details specific to Kerala.

Eligibility and Minimum Age

Applicants must be at least 16 years old to apply for a motorcycle learners licence without gears (with guardian consent) and 18 years old for other vehicle categories. The learner licence is valid for 6 months from the date of issuance, allowing ample time to prepare for the driving test.

Required Documents

Submit the following documents along with the application:

  • Proof of age (birth certificate, Aadhaar card, or school certificate)
  • Proof of address (Aadhaar card, utility bill, or ration card)
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Guardian consent letter (for applicants under 18)
  • Medical fitness certificate (if applicable)

Application Process

Applications are submitted online through the Parivahan Sarathi portal. After filling the form, schedule a slot at the nearest Kerala MVD office for document verification and biometric capture. Pay the applicable fees as per the current Motor Vehicles Act / Kerala MVD schedule (verify on the Parivahan portal).

Learners Test

The learners test assesses knowledge of traffic rules, road signs, and safe driving practices. It is conducted in Malayalam, English, or Hindi at designated RTOs. Candidates must answer correctly to pass; failing requires waiting and retaking the test.

Validity and Next Steps

The learner licence remains valid for 6 months. During this period, practice under supervision with a licensed driver. After passing the driving test, apply for a permanent licence to drive legally.

Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum age for a learners licence in Kerala?

The minimum age is 16 for motorcycles without gears (with guardian consent) and 18 for other vehicles.

How long is a Kerala learners licence valid?

A learners licence is valid for 6 months from the date of issuance.

Where can I apply for a learners licence?

Applications are made online via the Parivahan Sarathi portal, followed by a visit to the Kerala MVD office.

What documents are needed for a learners licence application?

Required documents include proof of age, address, photographs, guardian consent (for minors), and a medical fitness certificate if applicable.
